Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025‑09‑08

LifeUndo stores data locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local / browser.storage.local). It does not send data to any server.

Processed data

  • Recent text snippets you typed (last 20 states)
  • Recently closed tabs (URL, title, favicon) — last 10
  • Clipboard text history (based on copy selection) — last 10

Sharing

No data leaves your device. No analytics. No network requests.
