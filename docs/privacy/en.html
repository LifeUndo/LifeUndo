Privacy Policy

Updated: 2025-01-15

Privacy Principles

🏠 Local Data Storage

All your data (text, tabs, clipboard) is stored only in your browser

🚫 No Telemetry

The extension does not send any usage data

📊 No Analytics

We do not collect usage statistics or personal information

🔐 Offline Licenses

VIP licenses are validated locally via ECDSA P-256 without server requests

What We DON'T Collect

📝 Content

Content of your text or clipboard

🌐 URLs

URLs of visited pages

⏰ Usage Time

Usage time or frequency of actions

💻 Device Info

Device or browser information

📍 Geolocation

IP addresses or geolocation

Data Storage

All data is stored in browser's local storage (localStorage/sessionStorage) and can be cleared:

  • Through extension settings (Delete Key button)
  • Through browser settings (Clear Site Data)
  • When uninstalling the extension

GetLifeUndo Fund

10% of our net revenue goes to the digital literacy fund. This does not affect user privacy — the fund works with educational initiatives unrelated to user data.

Policy Changes

We will update this page for significant changes. Current version is always available on the website.
