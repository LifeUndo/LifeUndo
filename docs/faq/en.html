FAQ

What is LifeUndo?

A “second-chance” service in your browser: quickly restore your recent text, tabs, and clipboard.

Does the extension send telemetry?

No. Your data is stored locally in the browser. No telemetry by default.

How do I activate VIP?

Open the popup → “Activate VIP” → on the “License” page import your .lifelic. Verification is offline via ECDSA signature.

How do I switch language?

Use the extension settings to toggle RU/EN. The popup and the “What’s new” modal are localized.

What’s the difference between Free, Pro, and VIP?

Free — basic limits. Pro — increased limits. VIP — unlimited; when active, PRO prompts are hidden/disabled.

Which browsers are supported?

Firefox — available. Chrome/Edge — in preparation for listing.
