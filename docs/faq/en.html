Frequently Asked Questions

What is LifeUndo?

A second-chance service in your browser: quickly restore recent text, tabs, and clipboard. Everything works locally without sending data.

How does text undo work?

LifeUndo saves input history in text fields. If you accidentally deleted text, click the undo button or use hotkeys to restore it.

How to restore closed tabs?

The extension popup shows a list of recently closed tabs. Simply click on the tab you want to restore.

How does clipboard undo work?

The extension tracks text copying and saves clipboard history. You can go back to previously copied fragments.

Does the extension send my data?

No. Everything is stored locally in your browser. No telemetry, analytics, or data transmission to servers.

How to activate VIP license?

Open popup → "Activate VIP" → on the License page import .lifelic file. Validation happens offline via ECDSA.

How to switch interface language?

In the extension popup, click EN/RU buttons. Language is saved and applied to all interface elements.

Free vs Pro vs VIP differences?

Free: Basic limits (text 20, tabs 5, clipboard 5)
Pro: Extended limits (text 200, tabs 50, clipboard 50)
VIP: Unlimited + lifetime license
