FAQ

What is LifeUndo?

A second-chance service in your browser: quickly restore your recent text, tabs, and clipboard.

Does the extension send telemetry?

No. Your data is stored locally in the browser.

How do I activate VIP?

Open the popup → “Activate VIP” → import .lifelic on the License page (offline ECDSA verification).

How do I switch language?

Use the extension settings to toggle RU/EN. Popup and “What’s new” are localized.

Free vs Pro vs VIP?

Free — basic limits. Pro — increased. VIP — unlimited; PRO prompts are hidden when active.

Supported browsers?

Firefox — available. Chrome/Edge — in preparation.
