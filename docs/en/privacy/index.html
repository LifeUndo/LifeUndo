LifeUndo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-08

Summary

  • All data is stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local/browser.storage.local).
  • No telemetry, analytics, ads or network requests.
  • The extension works offline.

Data we process

  • Text history — last 20 (Free) / 200 (Pro) states.
  • Recently closed tabs — last 10 (Free) / 50 (Pro) with URL, title and favicon.
  • Clipboard history — last 10 (Free) / 50 (Pro) text snippets.
  • Local statistics — counters (popup/undo/restore) stored locally for your convenience.
  • License — offline license file/token used to validate Pro/Team locally.

You can delete all data anytime using your browser or the extension’s UI.

Licensing & Trial

  • 7-day trial starts on first launch and is stored locally (with a helper flag in storage.sync).
  • Offline license — .lifelic file or token signed by us (ECDSA P-256). Verification is fully local.
  • License may include: plan, expires, seats, email. Used only locally to show status.

Browser permissions

The extension requests only minimal permissions needed for its functionality (local storage). You can review the list on your browser’s extensions page.

Cookies & Analytics

We do not use cookies or external analytics. The website may link to third-party stores (e.g., Firefox Add-ons); visiting them means you follow their policies.
