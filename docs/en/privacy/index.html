Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-08

Summary

LifeUndo stores data locally in your browser (chrome.storage.local / browser.storage.local). The extension does not send data to servers, does not make network requests and does not use analytics/telemetry.

Data we process

  • Text history — last 20 (Free) / 200 (Pro) states.
  • Recently closed tabs — up to 10 (Free) / 50 (Pro): URL, title and favicon.
  • Clipboard history — up to 10 (Free) / 50 (Pro) text items.
  • Local stats (optional) — counters of popup/undo/restore actions.

All items are stored only on your device. You can clear them in the UI or remove the extension, and the browser will delete the storage.

Storage & retention

  • Data lives in the browser’s local storage until you delete it or history limits are reached.
  • Oldest items are rotated out by a ring-buffer strategy.

Security

  • No network data exchange — fewer leak vectors.
  • Local storage is protected by the browser and extension permissions.
  • We don’t request site permissions beyond what Undo features require.

Pro licenses

License validation is offline. The key is stored locally and can be removed by you at any time in the license panel.

Your choices & control

  • Clear text/tabs/clipboard history in the UI.
  • Export/Import (Pro) — your archive stays with you.
  • Remove the extension — the browser deletes local data.

Contact

Privacy questions: developer@lifeundo.ru. News & support: Telegram.
