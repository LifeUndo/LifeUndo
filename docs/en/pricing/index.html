Frequently Asked Questions

How does the trial work?

After installing the extension, you automatically get 7-day access to all Pro features. No credit card required.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. After the paid period ends, the extension will return to the Free plan.

How do I pay?

We accept cards, e-wallets and other methods through Stripe/Paddle. Payment is secure and protected.

What happens to my data?

All data stays on your device. We don't have access to your text, tabs or clipboard.
