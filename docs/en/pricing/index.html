Frequently Asked Questions

How does the trial work?

After installing the extension, you automatically get 7-day access to all Pro features. No credit card required.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. After the paid period ends, the extension returns to the Free plan.

How does payment work?

We accept cards, e-wallets and other methods through Stripe/Paddle. Payment is secure and protected.

What happens to my data?

All data stays on your device. We have no access to your text, tabs or clipboard.
