GetLifeUndo Fund — 10% for Digital Literacy & Privacy

We commit to allocate 10% of our net revenue to education and privacy-first engineering initiatives. No politics. Transparent reporting.

"Net revenue" = gross revenue minus payment processor fees, taxes and refunds.

How it works

  • 10% of net revenue, calculated quarterly.
  • Quarterly reports (PDF) plus a short summary on this page.
  • Support via official agreements (schools/universities/NGOs/developers).
  • No politics — only education, research and open-source.

Focus areas

Privacy for students

Short lessons for kids and parents: passwords, phishing, cookies, why "local first" is safer.

What we fund
  • Mini-course (4–6 lessons) with decks and short videos
  • Posters/handouts for schools
  • Guides for classroom talks
Who & outcomes

Schools, after-school programs, NGOs. Outcomes: PDF guide, 6 short videos, 3 A3 posters, pilot report.

UI/UX for privacy

We support projects designing interfaces without tracking and heavy telemetry.

What we fund
  • Course/graduate projects
  • Interfaces that work without tracking
  • Research on wording/iconography explaining privacy
Who & outcomes

Students/researchers, UX labs. Outcomes: Figma prototype, article, open "privacy-by-design for forms" guide.

Open-source for offline data

Tools that work without the cloud: fewer leaks, less third-party dependency.

What we fund
  • Local clipboard/notes managers
  • Offline encryption and local backups
  • Input-recovery tools for offline use
Who & outcomes

OSS developers/teams (MIT/BSD/Apache). Outcomes: GitHub repo, release, video demo, local test guide.

How to apply

Prepare a short description (up to 1000 chars), plan/budget, timeline, what will be public (code/materials), link to your profile/organization.

Apply

Evaluation criteria: usefulness and clarity, openness, realistic budget/timing, fit with "local & private", no politics.

Reporting

Each quarter we publish a PDF report and a short summary: amounts, supported projects, links to outcomes. No personal data.
