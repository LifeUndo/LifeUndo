Frequently Asked Questions

What is "Undo"?

Undo means reverting your last action. In LifeUndo it's a quick rollback of text, closed tabs and clipboard when something goes wrong.

What are Free vs Pro limits?

Free: 20 text states, 10 tabs, 10 clipboard items.
Pro: 200 text states, 50 tabs, 50 clipboard items.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally in your browser (browser.storage.local). No servers, no network requests. Data stays only on your device.

Does it work offline?

Yes! LifeUndo works completely offline. Internet is only needed for extension installation and updates.

Can I recover emails or payments?

No, LifeUndo only works with local browser content. We cannot recover sent emails or cancel payments — only what you typed in website forms.

How to get Pro version?

Pro licenses are sold through Gumroad. After purchase you'll receive a .lifelic file for activation in extension settings.

Is it safe?

Yes, completely safe. All data stays on your device, we don't collect any information. The extension passed Mozilla AMO review.
