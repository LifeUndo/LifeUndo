💳 Payment and Licenses

No license email received

If you paid for Pro/VIP and didn't receive a license email within 1 hour:

  • Check your Spam folder
  • Contact support, specifying your email and plan (Pro/VIP)
  • Include approximate payment time

Payment issues

If you encountered problems paying through FreeKassa:

  • Try a different payment method
  • Check your card details
  • Contact FreeKassa support

How to get quick help

1. Describe the problem

Specify browser version, extension version and describe what exactly isn't working.

2. Attach screenshots

If possible, take a screenshot of the error or problematic situation.

3. Check logs

Open Developer Tools (F12) and check the console for errors.
