# GetLifeUndo — bring back lost text, tabs, clipboard and screenshots

GetLifeUndo saves your work in real time: recovers forms and text, closed tabs history, clipboard and a local screenshots archive. 100% local — no cloud, no tracking, no telemetry. Your data stays on your device.

## Features
- Real‑time form & text recovery
- Closed tabs history and restore
- Clipboard history
- Local screenshots archive
- Privacy by default: no data leaves your device
